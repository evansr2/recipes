Ingredients:
- 2 avocados
- 1 lime
- 2 tsp salt
- jalapeno to taste
- 1/2 onion
- tomato
 
Instructions:
- cut and mash avocados
- chop onion
- chop jalapeno
- chop tomato
- squeeze lime
- add salt
- and mix well
