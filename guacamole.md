Ingredients:
- 2 avocados
- 1 lime
- 2 tsp salt
- jalapeno to taste
- 1/2 onion
- 1/2 tomato, chopped
- 1 clove garlic, crushed
- 2 tbl minced cilantro
 
Instructions:
- cut and mash avocados
- chop onion
- chop jalapeno
- squeeze lime
- add salt
- add chopped tomatoes, crushed garlic and minced cilantro
- and mix well
